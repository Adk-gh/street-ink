About Street & Ink

Our Mission

Street & Ink was born from a passion for urban art and a desire to create a comprehensive platform where street art enthusiasts could discover, document, and discuss artworks from around the world.

We believe street art is a vital form of contemporary cultural expression that deserves to be preserved and celebrated. Our platform serves as both an archive and a community for those who appreciate the creativity found on city walls.

Founded in 2018 by a group of photographers, artists, and technologists, Street & Ink has grown into the largest crowdsourced street art database with contributions from over 80 countries.
